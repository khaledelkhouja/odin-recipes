Chicken Brick

Chicken Brick?

Do you want to prepare an original dish for your guests, easy to make? So we invite you to discover this family recipe from Tunisia. The bricks are very often revisited: with chicken, cow kiri, vegetables... We offer you a colorful recipe, which will delight your taste buds; the delicious chicken and cheese bricks.

Chicken bricks or briouates, golden brown and crispy, perfect for a starter or an aperitif with friends.

Ingredients

  • 200 gr of chicken
  • 8 sheets bricks
  • 100 gr grated cheese Preferably Emmental
  • 1 onion
  • 1 egg
  • 2 peppers 1 red & 1 yellow
  • Olive oil

Preparation steps

Preparation: 20 min

Cooking: 15 min

  1. Start by frying the onion and peppers, cut into small squares, in a drizzle of olive oil
  2. Once they have returned, add the chicken cut into small pieces and mix lightly so that it is tapered.
  3. Add salt, pepper, sweet paprika and cinnamon.
  4. Pour a little water so that the mixture is not too dry.
  5. Once the chicken is cooked, add the egg and mix to bind all the ingredients.
  6. Then place your mixture on Â¼ of a pastry sheet. Add a handful of grated Emmental and fold it over on itself to form a triangle.
  7. In a frying pan, add frying oil and fry your bricks.
  8. Once nicely browned, take them out and pat them dry with paper towel.
